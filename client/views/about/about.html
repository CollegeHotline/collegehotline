Our Story

Born out of need. In California alone, there are 945 students for every guidance counselor.

We Are

Students

Educators

Innovators

With a dizzying amount of information on the internet, we want to create an easy and quick way for students to get personalized answers to their questions. Student phone calls and text messages are directed to the browsers of volunteers who are college students from the surrounding region. This way, we hope to tap into the diverse knowledge and experience of those who made it through the college maze successfully. From college applications and personal statements, to FAFSA, and how to pick a major, let the College Hotline be your personal guide on the path to college.
